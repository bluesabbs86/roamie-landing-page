

## Roamie Landing Page

A vibrant, warm landing page for the Roamie itinerary planning app using coral/sunset orange tones with clean white.

### Color Theme
- Primary: Warm coral (#FF6B6B) and sunset orange (#FF8E53)
- Accents: Deep coral for CTAs, soft peach for backgrounds
- Clean white sections with warm neutral text

### Sections

1. **Hero** — Full-width gradient background (coral → orange). Large headline with tagline "Your budget. Your adventure." Subtext explaining the value prop. Two CTAs: "Start Planning" (filled) and "See How It Works" (outline). Illustration/mockup placeholder of the app.

2. **Features** — Three-column grid with icons:
   - Smart Budget Tracking
   - AI-Powered Itineraries  
   - Local Deal Finder

3. **How It Works** — Three-step visual flow: Set your budget → Get personalized plans → Book & go

4. **Testimonials** — Carousel of traveler quotes with avatars, warm-toned cards

5. **CTA Section** — Bold gradient banner: "Go further for less." with email signup / waitlist form

6. **Footer** — Links, social icons, brand logo

### Design Details
- Friendly, rounded typography and soft shadows
- Subtle travel-themed decorative elements (map pins, dotted travel lines)
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll animations on section entry
- Smiling map pin as the brand icon/logo mark
